Apps

School Bullying APP: AnonymousShare

I designed this app to thwart school bullying with other 3 friends. Basically, all the users information in this app are anonymous. So the people can share their experiences and feeling about school bullying in this app without disclosing personal information. The other users can also comment anonymously below to encourage the victims. I hope that people in my school can feel more supported when they face this issue.

App Making:What I have learned?

Making this app improves my communication skills and leadership, because this is the first time I launch a computer science project as the leader. During this process, I think I get to know that a good leader should not only have technical skills, but also the ability to connect all the group members together.

We met many challenges, because this project is far more than coding. We made a survey to understand the school bullying problem first, and then wrote plan about how to improve functions, and designed the prototype of app on paper, etc. However, it was a lot of fun, because of my team’s passions and supports.

Admittedly, the app is very plain. Because the limitation of our skills in grade 11, we used Thunkable to design it. However, I am interested in expanding this project. I am self-studying Javascript now. So my team member and I will start to make a new version(React Native APP) by using Javascript the next year.

Game

Java Game: Escaping from earth

This is my first game project and also my final project for AP CS class.

Honestly, my teacher didn’t expect us to make this game at that time, because he didn’t teach us the graphic tools for designing games. However, I was very interested in designing games, so I just self-studied graphics in Java online. Last year, I became one of the only two students who made game as the final project in my class.

Self-studying was really struggling, because I didn’t have any instructors. What I could rely on was only some tutorial videos online and myself. Though this game was really simple, it took me a month to complete this project. However, I am still glad I did that. Because during this process, I got to be more familiar with applying concepts I learned in class like loops, arrays, class, etc.

Overall, this project had great influence on my attitude towards how to study Computer Science. In the past, I just listened to the class and finished the class labs. However, after making this game, I was inspired to continue studying more things and applying them in the real world!
