Tutoring Experience

Helping others be more confident people and find their passion is always my wish.

BaiNian Technology Education Program: TA

Though I have done other tutoring before, this tutoring experience is very important to me. It was when my affinity to STEM Education originated.

I worked as a teacher assistant in this program to teach 20+ vocational school students how to build their own websites. Before, most of them didn’t receive any training on computer science and knew few English. When they clumsily typed on keyboard using two fingers, there were stars in their eyes. I was moved by their concentrations.

When I talked with one of the students, I found that we are both really into folk music, so I encouraged him to design a folk music website. While coding, I saw his body followed the rhythm of the music he was playing. That was very cute. At that moment, I was sure that I saw the passion burned in his body. His work ended up being put at the top to display.

I believe this program helps some of those students interested in technology. Even not, making their own music, news, dancing, or TV series websites must be a very cool thing in their life. I was very fulfilled to be the person bringing these to them.

Girls Who Tech:Organizer

I have long concerned the gender equality in STEM field. Actually, I have been discouraged to study computer science before. Inspired by my own experience, I organized this Girls Who Code Program to help more girls like me be confident about studying STEM.

During this program, I met many girls with creative ideas, especially a 12-year-old girl (She is the youngest attendee). We chatted a lot about her experience in robotics. I was really content to not only help others, but to get along with so many friends who have the same interests as me.

My friends and I plan to organize more programs like this in 2020. I am very thrilled for helping various people and creating innovative projects in the future.

School English Tutoring Club: President & Teacher

I tutored in English Tutoring and also Chinese tutoring clubs for two years. Now, I am the president and the teacher of my school’s Eureka English tutoring clubs. Basically, I work with my literature teacher to help grade 10 students practice their English in the leisure time.

During this process, I learned how to prepare classes materials, plan interesting class activities, interact with students, etc. I’d say the school tutoring experience is so important that it sets the foundation for my interests in education.
