Statistic Project

Carleton College Summer Program Project: Is Freedom Money?

I did this statistics research in Carleton College Summer Quantitative Reasoning Program.

I really like this interdisciplinary research which I combined statistics methods with politics to examine the relationship among social liberties and income inequality.

In this project, my group members and I gathered data from more than 20+ database. Our final analysis included the data of 100+ countries.
